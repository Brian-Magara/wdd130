Whitewater Rafting

Our mission is to provide unforgettable rafting experiences that connect people with nature and adventure. We believe in safety, fun, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2005 by Brian Magara, Whitewater Rafting began as a small family operation with just two rafts. What started as a passion for river adventures grew into one of the region's most trusted rafting companies.

Today, we operate on five major rivers with a fleet of over 20 rafts and a team of certified guides. We're proud to have introduced thousands of adventurers to the thrill of whitewater rafting while maintaining our commitment to safety and environmental stewardship.
